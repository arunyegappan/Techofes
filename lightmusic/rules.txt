1.	There should be a minimum of 3 members and a maximum of 12 members in a band with at least 3 members performing each song.<br>
2.	Songs should be in Indian languages. Minor inclusions of other languages in a song allowed.<br>
3.	More than one instrumental piece not allowed in a band's performance.<br>
4.	Own compositions will be given additional weightage.<br>
5.	Bands must be registered beforehand. Any band registering on-the-spot will be given the last slot for their performance. If there is a time constraint, they MIGHT not be allowed to perform.
