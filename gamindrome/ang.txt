1.Game will be conducted as FOUR sessions.<br>
2.Each session will have different levels, that will be selected by the organiser.<br>
3.All contestants will be given only one chance to set his/her best score.<br>
4.At the end of each session the contestant with highscore is selected to the final level.<br>
5.In finals the contestant will given TWO chances to set his/her best score.<br>
6.The contestant with High score is announced as the WINNER.
