<b>Ashok Saravanan</b> (Ayashok) and <b>Arun Titan</b>.<br>
Two men who held numerous exhibitions, had their photographs reproduced countless times and been interviewed by the photography community at large.
Initially a Graphic Designer, their everyday work in editing photographs got them curious about shapes, pictures & colors.. As their passion for pictures increased, so did their passion for photography. <br>
